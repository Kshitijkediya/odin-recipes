Classic Veggie Stir-Fry

It shows a mixture of vegetables neatly arranged in the bowl it shows a wooden spoon which is probably used to make the dish

Ingridients:

  • chopped mixed veggies
  • soy sauce
  • cloves minced garlic
  • cornstarch
  • oil
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add the harder vegetables (carrots, broccoli) and stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Toss in the bell peppers and garlic; cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and the cornstarch slurry
  5. Stir continuously for 1 minute until the sauce thickens and coats the veggies. Serve over rice.
